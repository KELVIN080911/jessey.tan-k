JESSEY'S FAVOURITE FRUITS

1. Passion Fruit

-The flavor is described as citrusy with hints of melon, pineapple, or kiwi. As the fruit ripens, it increases in sweetness, but more importantly, complexity.



2. Blueberry

-Blueberries have a slightly sweet taste with a bit of acid.



3. Pomegranate

-Pomegranate is a fruit that is sweet and tart with a thick skin.
